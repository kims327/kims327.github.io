How I Organize Photos

I never backup my phone or archive digital images to my desktop like others do. Even though I've been using iphone for several years, I still don't prefer using icloud since I find it very confusing. So I tend to keep all my digital images on my phone in Photos just the way they are.

I would say the most important file from my Photos album would be 'favorites' album. I am a type of person who take 100 pictures at a time so I always end up with this massive scale album that contains over 19,000 pictures. 'Favorites' is a special album where I only pick images that I really love among 19,000 pictures. The number gets bigger as more pictures get accumulated to this Photos album but I would still say 'favorites' is the most inportant album for me since it contains images that I never want to lose.

Digital VS. Physical

Organizing digitally Organizing physically
Organizing digitally makes everything faster. Could be a little slower process than digital archives.
However, organizing digitally makes things to get lost so easily since the scale of images that can be archived is different from archiving things physically. Easy to remember where you put certain things in the past.
