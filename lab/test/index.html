How to make Fruit Smoothie

Puree vanilla yogurt,
frozen strawberries, frozen banana and orange juice in a blender until smooth.

How to make Banana Chips

Slice a banana into rounds and lay on a greased baking sheet. Bake at degrees F until golden, hours. Let harden at room temperature.

How to make Caramel Popcorn

Melt butter with honey and of salt. Toss with popcorn and pecans. Spread on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake at degrees F, tossing. Cool.
